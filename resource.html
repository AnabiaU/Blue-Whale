Blue Whale

Natural Recources Inventory

Important Attributes Explanation
Water Water is important for Blue Whales because sea mammals have a thick layer of blubber (Fat). And lack of water can cause the Blue Whale to overheat and get dehydrated.
Food Food is crucial for the survival of every animal. Food is needed for nutrients and for growth and repair of the body. “If you’re a very large animal, you have a lot of body mass that you have to maintain,” says marine biologist Jeremy Goldbogen.(By Polly Shulman - American museum of natural history)
Shelter/Protection Protection is important for Blue Whales because their waste is very rich in iron and is a major source of nutrients in the marine food chain, increasing productivity in the Southern Ocean, which boosts fishery yields (Blue Whale)
Nutrients Nutrients are an essential part of growth. Nutrients help organism to be healthy and reproduce.
Air Air is very important for all organisms. It provides everyone energy to eat, grow, and live.
